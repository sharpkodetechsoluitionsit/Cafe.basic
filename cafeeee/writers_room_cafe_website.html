Our Story

A hilltop escape for creators and coffee lovers

Perched above the shimmering Rushikonda coastline on Ramanaidu Studios Road, The Writer's Room was born from a simple belief — that great ideas deserve great coffee, and both deserve a breathtaking view.

We are more than a café. We are a sanctuary for the creative soul. A workspace, a date destination, a social ritual — where the sea breeze and the scent of freshly brewed espresso conspire to unlock something extraordinary in you.

Our hilltop setting, curated ambience, and premium culinary craft make every visit feel like the opening scene of something beautiful.

The Experience

01

Sea View Terraces

Every seat tells a story of the horizon. Panoramic views of the Bay of Bengal, framed by our open terraces and ambient lighting.

02

Sunset Evenings

Watch the Rushikonda sky transform into liquid gold each evening. Our hilltop position makes every sunset a private show curated just for you.

03

Creative Workspace

High-speed Wi-Fi, power points at every table, and a hushed atmosphere that invites deep work. Your most productive office has a sea view.

04

Coffee Culture

From single-origin cold brews to classic Americanos and our signature Irish Cold Coffee — each cup is a ritual, not just a beverage.

05

Romantic Ambience

Warm amber lighting, intimate corners, and the sound of waves below. The Writer's Room is Visakhapatnam's most romantic café destination.

06

Social Gatherings

Birthdays, creative meetups, team outings, or an impromptu afternoon with friends — our flexible spaces set the scene for your stories.
